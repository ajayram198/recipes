Recipe to make cheese bread omlette
1.Cut onion, green chillies, mushroom, tomatoes and capsicum, and put it into a tumbler.
2.Take two eggs and break them and put it into tumbler. Then whisk the contents.3.Pour this mixture into a frying pan and fry on both sides.
4.After this omlette is prepared, then take brown bread and cheese and put it on top of the omlette on one side.
5.Serve hot.
